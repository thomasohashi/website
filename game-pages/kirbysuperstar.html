KIRBY SUPER STAR

Developer: HAL LAboratory
Publisher: Nintendo
Platform: Super NES
Release: NA: September 20, 1996

Out of all the 2D Kirby games I still think this one feels the best. The controls are snappy and precise and the copy abilities have more nuance and variation than all of the games that have followed.

It's also an omnibus game!

In addition to a few traditional style Kirby games there are also two(!) search action Kirby games.
