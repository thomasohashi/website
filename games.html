Earthbound

Starmen.net

This game made me who I am. Maybe. Structurally it's a fairly conventional old school RPG but it's so much more than that. The world, writing, and characters are all weird, funny, and delightful. It's currently playable on Nintendo Switch but I think the Wii U promotional video they made is more interesting to watch.

ARCO

Official Website

Arco is a mesoamerican western revenge tactical RPG with super fresh feeling combat, beautiful pixel art, and excellent writing. I got destroyed by the demo I played at PAX 2023 but I can now proudly say that I "figured it out" and had a supremely satisfying time fighting my way through the game.


Puzzmo

Play Games

I kind of have a love-hate relationship with crosswords. Basically I love them until I get stumped by an esoteric clue that I have no context for and then I hate them. Puzzmo solves this problem in two ways.

First, and more importantly, it has a really well integrated hint system. Why has nobody ever done this before??? Second, it features puzzles made by people who use esoteric clues that I actually understand.

The other games are fun too, though I am better at some than others.

Mars After Midnight

Official Website

Hey do you know about the Playdate? It's a super cool handheld console that probably deserves its own page. Anyway, Mars After Midnight by Lucas Pope is a space rejection/support group management simulator with an absolutely banging theme song that I'm sharing here instead of a trailer.

I'm not going to say much more about it because I don't want to spoil anything and honestly it's kind of hard to describe anyway.
