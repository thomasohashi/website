COOL MUSIC

Bubble Blade

Bubble Blade, aka Grim father is a Portland(ish?) musician that makes cool dungeon synth music. Vaporous Halls has big RPG adventure energy but you kind of want to dance to it.

Hotel Pools

Hotel Pools is another Portland musician that creates relaxing and funky chill synth. If you ever need a vacation from life just put this on and close your eyes.

Serengeti

Serengeti, from Chicago, not Portland, has a long running series of concept albums centered around the fictional, washed up rapper Kenny Dennis. As a whole it is my favorite music art of all time but because of its length it can feel a bit daunting. Ajai introduces a new character and the production is incredible, so I think it's a pretty good entry point.

COOL GAMES

Earthbound

Starmen.net

One of the coolest games ever made. Structurally it's a fairly conventional old school RPG but it's so weird and wonderful. The world, writing, and characters are all out of this world. It's currently playable on Nintendo Switch.

ARCO

Official Website

Arco is a super fresh feeling tactical RPG with tight combat, beautiful art, and excellent writing. The story follows four characters in a modern take on a classic western revenge story with a mesoamerican twist.


Mars After Midnight

Official Website

Hey do you know about the Playdate? It's a super cool handheld console that probably deserves its own page. Anyway, Mars After Midnight by Lucas Pope is a space rejection/support group management simulator with an absolutely banging theme song that I'm sharing here instead of a trailer.
