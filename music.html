COOL MUSIC

Bubble Blade

Bubble Blade, aka Grim father is a Portland(ish?) musician that makes cool dungeon synth music. Vaporous Halls has big RPG adventure energy but you kind of want to dance to it.

Hotel Pools

Hotel Pools is another Portland musician that creates relaxing and funky chill synth. If you ever need a vacation from life just put this on and close your eyes.

Serengeti

Serengeti, from Chicago, not Portland, has a long running series of concept albums centered around the fictional, washed up rapper Kenny Dennis. As a whole it is my favorite music art of all time but because of its length it can feel a bit daunting. Ajai introduces a new character and the production is incredible, so I think it's a pretty good entry point.

Leon Chang

Leon Chang, from New York I guess, makes really cool video game inspired music. The world needs more fully realized fake video game soundtracks and Leon is picking up the rest of our slack.
