COOL MUSIC

Use With Speakers or Headphones

Electronic

Bubble Blade

Bubble Blade, aka Grim father is a Portland(ish?) musician that makes cool dungeon synth music. Vaporous Halls has big RPG adventure energy but you kind of want to dance to it.

Hotel Pools

Hotel Pools is another Portland musician that creates relaxing and funky chill synth. If you ever need a vacation from life just put this on and close your eyes.

Leon Chang

Leon Chang, from New York(?), makes really cool video game inspired music. Return to Bird World genuinely feels like the lost soundtrack to a Super Mario RPG style adventure.

Hip-Hop

Previous Industries

Previous Industries is made up of (also) Chicago rappers Open Mike Eagle, Video Dave, and Still Rift, and while this is their first album together their chemistry makes it very apparent that they've known each other for a long time. Together the three combine humor and melancholy with brilliant wordplay and a classic underground sound, resulting in an album is 100% guaranteed to put a backpack on your back.

Serengeti

Serengeti, from Chicago, not Portland, has a long running series of concept albums centered around the fictional, washed up rapper Kenny Dennis. As a whole it is my favorite music art of all time but because of its length it can be a bit daunting. Ajai is little more accessible as it introduces a new character to the saga and has great beats, so I think it's a good place to start for the uninitiated.
